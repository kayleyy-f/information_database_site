Overview

Orientation late July; classes begin first week of August. Suburban/rural campus in Greenville, NC on ECU Health Medical Center grounds. AMCAS deadline Nov 1; secondary Dec 15. Mission: increase supply of primary care physicians for NC, improve eastern NC health, and support minority/disadvantaged students.

Admissions

AMCAS primary by Nov 1 (no EDP). Rolling review July–spring; interviews by invitation (NC residents only) Sept–Feb. Secondary $100, due Dec 15. CASPer required.

Acceptance Data

Class size ~86, 100% NC residents. Avg MCAT ~509; avg GPA ~3.73. In-state applicant acceptance ~8.5% of those screened (out-of-state disallowed).

Education & Research

Systems-based preclinical with OMM, Longitudinal Integrated Clerkship option, community medicine focus. Required scholarly project; summer research stipend; tuition-free Brody Scholars full-ride program; strong primary care & rural health training.

Tuition, Aid & Debt

Annual tuition & fees ~$23,300 (same for all students). Full scholarships for Brody Scholars; ~50% receive some scholarship. Avg graduate debt ~$150,000. NC primary care forgivable loans available.

Campus Life

Close-knit “Brody family” on suburban campus. Affordable housing near campus; tight peer support; >40 orgs including Rural Health, SNMA, SGA; cadaver memorial & ball; intramurals; strong student-run free clinics; ECU Pirates football traditions.

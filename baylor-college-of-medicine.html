Overview

  • Classes begin: Mid-July (orientation mid-July; classes start late July).
  • Campus type: Urban (Houston’s Texas Medical Center).
  • Application deadline: Primary via TMDSAS due November 1; supplemental due November 15.
  • Mission: “Baylor College of Medicine is a health sciences university that creates knowledge and applies science and discoveries to further education, healthcare and community service locally and globally.”

Admissions

  • Primary application deadline: November 1 (TMDSAS, 5 p.m. CST).
  • Review begins: July (rolling through December).
  • Application system: TMDSAS (Texas Medical & Dental Schools Application Service).
  • Secondary application fee: $100 due November 15 (5 p.m. CST).

Acceptance Data

  • Median MCAT: ~518 (97th percentile).
  • Median GPA: ~3.9.

Education & Research

Baylor is research-intensive “dedicated to bringing discoveries from bench to bedside.” Opportunities include the SOAR program and a five-year Medical Research Pathway. Students are encouraged to engage in lab or clinical research, with mentorship via Texas Medical Center affiliations.

Tuition, Aid & Debt

  • Tuition & Fees: $26,622 in-state | $41,788 out-of-state (2025–26).
  • Combined degrees: MD/PhD, MD/MPH, MD/MBA.

Campus Life

  • Part of the world’s largest medical complex with simulation centers, libraries, and clinics.
  • 50+ student organizations: clinical interest, service, wellness, leadership.
  • Robust wellness programming and student-run free clinics.
